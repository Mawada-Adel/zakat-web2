Kaffarah (Expiation) Payment

Using Expiation (Kaffarah) eService, you can pay to fulfill the Kaffarah based on its type. Also, you can choose a specific state or district of the Sultanate where your money goes to.
You can pay the required amount online and it will reach the beneficiaries.

.su You can acquire the service from the portal: Zakah.om

NONE

About three minutes

The service is free. However, there could be fees by the bank.

The Kaffarah (Expiation) amount will be calculated automatically based on the type you select. Hence it's a fixed amount per type. Please note that the amount is calculated for ONE of the type.
