Sadaqah (Donation) Payment

Using Sadaqah (Donation) eService, you can pay your Sadaqah online and select which channel your money goes to. Also, you can choose a specific state or district of the Sultanate.
There are two types of Sadaqah:
1- Ongoing: which is used to finance sources and endowment assets.
2- General: it is given to beneficiaries directly through the system.
