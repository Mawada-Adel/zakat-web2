Zakah Calculator

Using Zakah Calculator eService, you; as Zakah payer, will be able to calculate your Zakah amount based on different applicable types:
- Money.
- Gold.
- Silver.
- Shares.
- Trade Offers.
- Crops.
- Cattle.

Steps for acquiring the service:
1. Select type/s of Zakah.
2. Enter the required information.
3. View the amount which is automatically calculated by the system.
4. You may pay online.


NONE

Instant service to calculate Zakah amount.
Note: you will need three minutes to pay online.

FREE

Please enter the values correctly for which it will affect the calculation results for Zakah amount, and you can benefit from the fatwa service in the system.
